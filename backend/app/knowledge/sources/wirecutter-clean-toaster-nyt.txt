Description (authoritative step list):
On behalf of your toaster, we're here to say: Please don't turn it upside down and shake the crumbs out. It's sensitive, and should be handled with care. Here's how to clean a toaster the right way.

You'll need:
- Pastry brush
- Long, thin paintbrush
- Dish cloths or paper towels
- Sponge
- Dish soap
- Bar Keepers Friend (for stainless steel toasters)

How to do it:
1. Unplug the toaster.
2. Use the pastry brush and paintbrush to gently brush crumbs away from the heating elements into the bottom.
3. Remove and empty the crumb tray. Wipe it with a damp paper towel or cloth and dry thoroughly before reinserting.
4. Carefully wipe the outside of the toaster with another damp towel. You can use a spot of dish soap and a sponge on tough stains, but careful not to get water in the slots, levers, or buttons.
5. Dry off the toaster.
6. If it's stainless steel, polish the exterior with Bar Keepers Friend.
